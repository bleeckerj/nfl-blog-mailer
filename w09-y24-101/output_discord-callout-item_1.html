The "Necessary Tomorrows" podcast, presented by Doha Debates and Al Jazeera Podcasts, blends speculative fiction with documentary elements, inviting listeners to explore potential futures shaped by the interactions between humans, artificial intelligence, and the natural world, as envisioned by science fiction writers and actualized by activists and scientists.
