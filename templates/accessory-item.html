Gnome School  ↻

@DrewWiberg

In your downtime between shifts at the algae farm, tune into Gnome School, helpfully sponsored by Borwick's Baking Powder, Sunlight Soap, and Oxo. Listening to Gnome School allows you to almost experience, from wherever you are in the climate wastes, the harmony of nature and spirit in homes strategically sited around symbolic mid-western mound placements featuring rooftop gardens and light-piping roofs.
