The Santiago Boys Podcast  ➜

In the 🎧-podcasts channel @Julian posted this

Listen to The Santiago Boys on Spotify. The Santiago Boys is a nine-part podcast about a group of radical utopians around Salvador Allende, Chile's socialist president. Undeterred by the Cold War and machinations of their enemies and aided by an eccentric British consultant, they try to wrestle control over technology from multinationals and intelligence agencies and use it to create a more egalitarian economy. As their dream gets crushed by Pinochet's bloody coup, the Santiago Boys find an unexpected afterlife - and in Silicon Valley of all places. The series is written and presented by Evgeny Morozov, a leading technology critic.

🧱 An artifact you find interesting
I have a reproduction of a medical clay tablet from Sumeria, 2400 BCE. It's remarkable because it still contains its data after all these millennia and is beautifully ergonomic at the human scale, unlike the compact computers we carry in our hands

📽 A film that has shaped or influenced who you are today
Blade Runner, 1982 Ridley Scott's Director's Cut: for its sci-fi objects and experiences, lighting and textures, and ultimately what it means to be human.

My father worked at NASA in Langley, Virginia, when NASA was established. He was a flight test engineer for the Mercury Mission and part of Gemini. He died at age 49. I have always been curious about his time at NASA. He was passionate about Sci-Fi, books, movies and shows. My mother often said that while watching Star Trek, he would comment on the mathematical possibilities of Star Trek creator Gene Roddenberry's inventions.

The Transmission

Upon detecting an anomalous narrowband signal at 2296.481481 MHz the DSS tracking station supervisor initiated a comprehensive investigation. Collaborative analysis with White Sands Station and Guam confirmed the signal's non-terrestrial origin. The transmission was decoded with a narrator describing a mysterious entity referred to as 'The Box.' This report encapsulates the findings and ongoing inquiries into this unprecedented cosmic phenomenon, urging the scientific community to explore the intricate tapestry of space-time signals.
